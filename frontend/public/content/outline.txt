Course Outline: Introduction to Data Science and AI - Essentials

Target Audience: Complete beginners to data science
Main Learning Outcome: Understand the basic concepts and terminology of data science and AI
Estimated Duration: 90-120 minutes (self-paced)


Module 1: Foundations of Data Science and AI (25 min)

* Lesson 1.1: What is Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI?

    ** Defining key terms and relationships
    ** The data science ecosystem
    ** Real-world applications

* Lesson 1.2: Data Strategy and Use Cases

    ** Why organizations need data strategies
    ** Common use cases (predictive maintenance, customer analytics, etc.)

* Knowledge Check: Quick Quiz (5 questions)


Module 2: The Data Science Team and Workflow (20 min)

* Lesson 2.1: Key Roles in Data Teams

    ** Data Scientists, Data Engineers, Data Analysts
    ** Subject Matter Experts and Business Analysts
    ** Solution Architects

* Lesson 2.2: From Data to Insights

    ** The analytical process
    ** Iterative exploration and implementation

* Knowledge Check: Quick Quiz (5 questions)


Module 3: Data Infrastructure Essentials (20 min)

* Lesson 3.1: Hardware and Cloud Computing Basics

    ** Understanding computational resources
    ** Why cloud platforms matter

* Lesson 3.2: Data Storage and Processing

    ** Data warehouses vs. data lakes
    ** Introduction to data pipelines

* Knowledge Check: Quick Quiz (5 questions)


Module 4: Putting It All Together (15 min)

* Lesson 4.1: Data Visualization and Communication

    Why visualization matters
    Best practices for presenting results

* Lesson 4.2: AI in Practice

    Industry applications
    Ethical considerations and trustworthy AI

* Knowledge Check: Quick Quiz (5 questions)


Final Assessment (10 min)

* Comprehensive quiz (15-20 questions)
* Scenario-based questions testing understanding


Learning Outcomes:
By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

Define data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence and explain their relationships
Identify key roles in data science teams and their responsibilities
Understand the basic data science workflow from strategy to implementation
Explain fundamental concepts in data infrastructure and processing
Describe common machine learning approaches and AI applications
